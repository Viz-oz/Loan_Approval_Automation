The loan approval prediction dataset contains the details of applicants who have applied for property loan.

The bank decides the approval of the loan based on factors such as applicant income, loan amount, credit history etc.

The goal of the project is to build a Machine Learning Model that succesfully makes decision regarding loan approval.

Data Attributes¶

Loan_ID: A unique loan ID

Gender: Gender of the Applicant

Married: Yes/No

Dependents: No.of dependants of the applicant

Education: Education ( Graduate/Undergraduate)

Self_Employed: Yes/No

ApplicantIncome: Income of the Applicant

CoapplicantIncome: Co-applicant income.

LoanAmount: Loan amount in thousands.

Loan_Amount_Term: Terms of the loan in months.

Credit_History: Credit history meets guidelines.

Property_Area: Applicants are living either Urban, Semi-Urban or Rural.

Loan_Status: Loan approved (Y/N)
